### Script 3: The "Get Ready With Me" (GRWM)
**Tone:** Soft, "Pinterest-inspired," and serene.
**Focus:** The sensory experience and natural ingredients.

* **Hook:** (Aesthetic shot of the product sitting next to some fresh leaves or flowers) "POV: You finally found a face wash that smells like a spa and actually works."
* **Problem:** "I hate that artificial, 'perfumey' smell most brands have. Plus, my skin is super sensitive, so I’m always scared to try new things."
* **Solution:** (User applying the foam/gel; slow motion shots) "This herbal blend is so soothing. No synthetic fragrances—just pure, botanical goodness. It feels so lightweight and leaves me feeling refreshed, not restricted." 
* **CTA:** "Treat your skin to some nature. Check out their latest bundle in the bio!"

### Expert Tips for Success:
* **The First 3 Seconds:** Ensure the text overlay matches the hook (e.g., "Stop stripping your skin!") to grab attention even if the sound is off.
* **Lighting:** Use natural sunlight near a window. It emphasizes the "Herbal/Natural" theme better than studio lights.
* **The "Texture" Shot:** People love seeing the product consistency. Make sure to get a clear shot of the gel or foam on your fingertips.
